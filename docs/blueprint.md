# **App Name**: VastraAI

## Core Features:

- AI Personality Stylist: Interactive AI quiz and camera integration to suggest personalized clothing styles based on user preferences and body type using generative AI.
- Sustainable Collections: Browse curated collections of sustainable clothing, with detailed information on materials and ethical production practices.
- Secure Shopping Cart: Secure and user-friendly shopping cart with multiple payment options.
- About Us: Detailed information about Vastra's mission, values, and commitment to sustainability.
- User Registration: Allow users to save shipping and payment information for quicker and easier purchases. Support saving items to user's own, personalized wishlist.
- Customer Support: Comprehensive customer support features, including a FAQ section, chatbot, email, and phone contact options.
- Customer Reviews: Enable both written and video customer reviews to enhance product credibility and community engagement. Includes a reporting tool for flagging inappropriate reviews.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy green (#8FBC8F) to reflect sustainability and nature.
- Background color: Warm beige (#F5F5DC), a desaturated shade of earthy green to create a cozy and inviting feel.
- Accent color: Muted brown (#B8860B), an analogous shade to the primary, for CTAs and important information.
- Body text: 'Literata' serif for readability and vintage flair.
- Headline Font: 'Playfair' sans-serif font for headlines, for a clean, high-end feel. Use Literata for body text
- Use natural and organic-themed icons to reinforce the brand's sustainable message.
- Clean and minimalist layout with ample white space to highlight products and enhance user experience.
- Subtle animations for product transitions and user interactions to create an engaging shopping experience.
